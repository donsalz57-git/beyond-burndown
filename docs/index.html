What Does the Feasibility Score Mean?

The score shows what percentage of planned work you can complete with available capacity.

80-100%
Healthy

You have enough capacity. The plan is realistic.

50-79%
At Risk

Capacity is tight. Consider reducing scope or adding resources.

0-49%
Overloaded

Not enough capacity. The plan needs significant changes.
