Understanding the Feasibility Score

The feasibility score quantifies how realistic your sprint plan is based on capacity vs demand analysis.

Feasibility Score Formula

Score = (Coverage Ratio - Overload Penalty) x 100

Coverage Ratio: How well capacity covers demand

Coverage Ratio = min(1, Total Capacity / Total Demand)

Overload Penalty: Penalizes days where demand exceeds capacity

Overload Penalty = min(0.3, (Overloaded Days / Total Days) x 0.5)

Score Interpretation

  • 80-100%: Healthy - Capacity exceeds demand with minimal overload periods
  • 50-79%: At Risk - Some overload periods exist or capacity is tight
  • 0-49%: Overloaded - Significant capacity shortage or many overloaded days

Completion Percentage

Tracks how much of the original planned work has been completed:

Completion % = (Cumulative Time Spent / Cumulative Original Estimate) x 100

This shows actual progress against what was originally planned, not remaining estimates which can change.
